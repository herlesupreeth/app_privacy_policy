Permissions, Information Collection and Use

For backwards compatability with Android 8.0 and 8.1 devices, I use the READ_PHONE_STATE permission. This permission allows me to access current Telephony subscriptions, Carrier Configurations on the phone and also to trigger changes to Carrier Configurations. However, this permission is not required on Android 9+ devices. The app itself does not have any mechanism to store any data and neither any mechanism to send any data. The information that I request is used just to display information on your device and is not collected by me in any way.
